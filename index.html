About Balatzis Katerini

Your Trusted Heavy Equipment Partner in Greece

Since 1985, Balatzis Katerini has been a leading provider of heavy equipment solutions throughout Greece. Based in Katerini with service centers across the country, we pride ourselves on our extensive inventory, technical expertise, and commitment to customer satisfaction.

Our team of experienced professionals ensures that every piece of equipment we sell meets the highest standards of performance and reliability, designed to withstand the demands of challenging work environments across various industries.

  • Over 35 years of experience in the Greek market
  • Nationwide service network
  • Comprehensive inventory of equipment and parts
  • Certified maintenance team

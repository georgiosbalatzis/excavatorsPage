About Us

Our Story

Balatzis Katerini was founded in 1985 with a vision to provide high-quality construction machinery and specialized services to the construction sector.

Based in Katerini and with more than 40 years of experience, we have evolved into a reliable partner for businesses and professionals throughout Greece.
